# Governance Spine — D-Project v1.0

The Governance Spine is the structural heart of the D-Project.
It guarantees fairness, legitimacy, and immunity to capture.

---

## 1. Coherence Governance Board (CGB)

### Purpose
Strategic decision-making, oversight, and structural governance.

### Properties
- Multi-stakeholder
- No actor > 40% control
- Staggered terms
- Transparent votes
- Publicly published summaries
- Mandatory rotation
- Distributed authority (civil society, workforce, academia, industry, state)

### Function
- Approve formulas
- Approve allocation frameworks
- Oversee compliance
- Trigger systemic reviews

---

## 2. Audit & Integrity Office (AIO)

### Purpose
Independent oversight of surplus measurements and compliance.

### Properties
- Firewalled funding
- Random audit assignment
- Rotating auditor corps
- Public audit reports
- Strict conflict-of-interest rules
- No private funding

### Function
- Validate surplus reporting
- Conduct randomized audits
- Publish summaries
- Trigger sanctions

---

## 3. Legal Compliance & Sanctions Framework (LCSF)

### Purpose
Ensure participation, compliance, and enforceability.

### Properties
- Statutory participation
- Non-regression clauses
- Automatic penalties
- Judicial appeal rights
- Enforcement based on employment insurance/pensions models

---

**Together, these three components form the system’s unbreakable spine.**
