# Boundary Architecture — D-Project v1.0

This defines who participates in the D-Project and who does not.

---

## 1. Included Actors

### 1.1 AI-Intensive Firms
Firms using GPAI or automation at scale.

### 1.2 Firms with Measurable AI Surplus
Any company with detectable post-AI profit delta.

### 1.3 Public-Sector AI Systems
Government systems generating measurable AI efficiency.

### 1.4 Multinationals Operating In-Jurisdiction
Participation based on local surplus, not corporate nationality.

---

## 2. Excluded Actors

### 2.1 Firms with No Measurable AI Surplus
No signal → no contribution.

### 2.2 Micro-Enterprises
<10 employees OR < \$250k revenue.

### 2.3 Collapsed States / War Zones
Governance stability required.

### 2.4 Non-Participating Countries
No cross-border enforcement.

### 2.5 Individuals
The D-Project never taxes people.

---

**The boundary model ensures fairness, political viability, and operational clarity.**
