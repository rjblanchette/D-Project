# Implementation Roadmap — D-Project v1.0

A staged, internationally compatible path for adoption.

---

## 1. Pilot Phase (12–18 months)

- 5–15 firms
- 20–40% randomly audited
- Sector-specific rules developed
- Updated models and equations
- Final Pilot Evaluation Report

---

## 2. National Rollout

- Enact legislation
- Establish CGB and AIO
- Integrate NADA into finance ministry
- Launch transparency dashboard
- Staged adoption across sectors
- National KPIs and annual reporting

---

## 3. International Harmonization

- Cross-border surplus alignment
- Multinational reporting standards
- ISO/OECD/IMF collaboration
- Treaty-adjacent adoption
- 3-year global review cycles

---

**The roadmap converts architecture into functioning public infrastructure.**
