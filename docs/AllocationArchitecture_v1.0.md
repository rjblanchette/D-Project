Defines how AI surplus moves from measurement to public benefit.

---

## 1. Five Stages

1. Surplus measured
2. Surplus audited
3. Collected into NADA
4. Allocated through protected channels
5. Distributed to programs and institutions

---

## 2. Protected Channels

### 2.1 Human Capability
Education, training, reskilling, creativity, digital access.

### 2.2 Time & Wellbeing
Reduced work-time pilots, care support, burnout reduction.

### 2.3 Civic & Cultural Infrastructure
Libraries, arts, community institutions, digital commons.

### 2.4 Structural Equity & Public Goods
Long-term equity, environmental projects, stability measures.

---

## 3. Strict Exclusions
Funds **cannot** be used for:
- defense
- political campaigns
- philanthropic foundations
- general budgets
- foreign aid

---

**This architecture prevents political diversion and maintains legitimacy.**
